Types of product

HPL Sheets

HPL sheets are used for furniture, doors, and wall panels due to their durability, and resistance to scratches, heat, and moisture.

Compact laminate

Compact laminate is used in high-moisture, high-traffic areas for its strength and resistance to damage.

postforming laminates

Postforming laminate is a bendable surface material ideal for smooth, seamless edges, often used in countertops, desks, and furniture for its durability and clean look

HPL Double Face(fiber board)

HPL Double Face is used in kitchens, wardrobes, and office furniture for its durability and matching double-sided finish.
