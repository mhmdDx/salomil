What We Do

HPL

HPL is a highly durable surface material crafted by compressing multiple layers of kraft paper soaked in resin under high heat and pressure. Its top layer is a decorative sheet protected by a transparent overlay, giving it excellent resistance to scratches, stains, and moisture. HPL is widely used in furniture, wall cladding, and countertops due to its versatility, stylish designs, and long-lasting properties. It’s ideal for areas with heavy wear and tear, making it a popular choice in both residential and commercial projects.

hpl double face (fiber board)

FIBER BOARD HPL is the nickname for a high-quality material designed especially for use in aluminum kitchens. Known for its durability, smooth finish, and stylish appearance, this innovative product combines the strength of fiberboard with the versatile surface of High-Pressure Laminate (HPL). It is available in a wide range of colors, making it an ideal choice for modern kitchen designs that demand both functionality and aesthetic appeal. FIBER BOARD HPL offers a practical and visually appealing solution for cabinetry, panels, and decorative elements in aluminum kitchen setups.

Compact

Compact boards, or compact laminates, are solid, self-supporting panels made from layers of kraft paper impregnated with phenolic resin, topped with decorative laminate layers. These boards are denser and thicker than regular laminates, offering enhanced strength, water resistance, and antibacterial properties. Compact boards are widely used in high-traffic areas, including toilet cubicles, lockers, and partitions, thanks to their superior durability, aesthetic appeal, and ability to withstand harsh environments.
