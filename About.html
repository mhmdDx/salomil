About Us

Salomil History

One of the few companies that took the challenge to produce the HPL laminate in the Middle East, Salomil company was the first producer to introduce the laminate in the Egypt since 1982.

Owned and managed by a group of foreign and Egyptian investors, Salomil company was the only producer of laminates that has maintained the upfront position as a leader in the market.

Our key of success

Since the beginning, We believed in each and every member of Salomil’s family which led us to improve day after day and gain the respect of each and every valuable customer we had the honor to serve.

Improving our quality standards and enlarging our products range were the main keys to enter and secure our presence in the Arab, African and European markets.

A combination of experience and new generation ideas of our old and new staff led us to rise our collaboration with our customers to a level of partnership.

our mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality products and services, while also ensuring that we operate in a sustainable and environmentally responsible manner. We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation, and we strive to exceed our customers' expectations in every aspect of our business.

our vision

Our vision is to be the leading provider of formica sheets products in the world, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability. We aim to be the preferred partner for our customers, providing them with the best products and services to meet their needs.
